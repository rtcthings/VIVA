What is VIVA?

A New Way to Give, Together.

VIVA (Vegas Investing in Visionary Action) is a high-energy, live event where Southern Nevada nonprofits present bold solutions and local funders commit support on the spot. Unlike traditional, competitive grantmaking, VIVA aligns partners around shared goals, turning inspiration into action.

Why It Matters?

Reimagining How Funding Works

Traditional funding is often slow, siloed, and paperwork-heavy. VIVA brings funders and nonprofits together to collaborate in real time — pooling resources and streamlining the process to focus on near-term milestones. The result? Promising ideas grow faster, partnerships strengthen, and the entire community benefits.

How It Works

An Evening of Energy, Purpose, and Impact

An evening emceed by Mark Shunock, filled with high energy and community spirit. Lightning-style pitches from local nonprofit organizations. Nonprofits presenting their “why,” the problem they’re solving, their innovative approach, proof of impact, and their vision.
