Real Stories of Local Impact

Hear from one of the nonprofits that received funding through our VIVA 2025. Their story reflects the heart of what VIVA is all about.

"Being part of VIVA 2025 was an incredible experience for our team at Lighthouse Charities. The event gave us more than funding, it connected us with people who genuinely care about creating change in Las Vegas. With the support we received, we've been able to expand our refugee job training program and help more families build new beginnings. VIVA reminded us that when a community comes together, anything is possible."

— Cindy, Executive Director, Lighthouse Charities
