What Makes VIVA Different?

VIVA is more than a pitch event—it's a movement. A gathering of generous, visionary individuals coming together in Las Vegas to fuel community-rooted ideas pitched by local nonprofits, and reimagine what philanthropy can feel like. VIVA invites funders to the table not just to judge, but to connect, to collaborate, and to invest with heart. This is not a high-stakes showdown. It's a vibrant evening of shared purpose, and yes; strategic giving.
